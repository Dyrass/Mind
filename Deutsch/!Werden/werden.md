## Meaning (bedeutet):
1. will be + Verb
2. will + verb
3. become + verb
4. will become + verb 
5. be 
# worden
ein form von dem inf. werden .
`geworden` ist die Partizip-II von dem `werden`
Ich werde Lehrer -> `in the process of smth , result will be in the future`
Ich bin Lehrer geworden -> `have completed the process and is the after result of smth that started in the Vergangenheit`

`ist + Geworden` , 
`worden` is an variant of the `geworden` which is used mainly in Passiv voice.
# würden
would ..... verb 
never comes alone usually with other verb 
```
würden + gehen = would go / would be going 
```

|Expression|Meaning|Tone|
|---|---|---|
|**Ich möchte gern...**|I would like...|Standard, natural ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐|
|**Ich würde gern...**|I would like to...|Slightly more tentative/polite ⭐⭐⭐⭐☆|
