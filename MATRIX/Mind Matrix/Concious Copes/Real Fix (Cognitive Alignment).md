There is only one way to actually _cure_ [[cognitive dissonance]] instead of just masking it: **You have to close the gap.** This requires radical honesty and effort.
```
[ Your Beliefs ]  <====== (Align Them) ======>  [ Your Actions ]
```
- **Behavioral Change:** If you believe smoking is bad, you quit. If you believe procrastination is ruining your life, you build a discipline system to stop. Your actions change to match your values.
- **Acknowledge and Update:** If your results don't match your thoughts, you update your thoughts based on evidence. You say, _"I thought I was great at this skill, but the data shows I'm struggling. I need to accept that I am a beginner and actually learn."_
---
Admitting you cannot change the outcome, dropping the "what ifs," and sitting with the raw truth.
Changing your actual actions to match your deeply held values.
